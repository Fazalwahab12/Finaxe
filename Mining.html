Mining and Metals

Expert Recruitment for Mining and Metals Operations

Finaxe provides specialized recruitment services for the mining and metals industry, covering every stage from exploration and extraction to smelting and ore processing. We help companies build efficient, safe, and innovative teams to support operations in this critical sector. .

Exploration & Development:

Geologists: Assessing mineral deposits and conducting surveys.

Mining Engineers: Designing and optimizing mining operations.

Project Managers:Leading exploration and development projects.

Mining Operations & Extraction:

Site Managers: Overseeing day-to-day mining operations and safety.

Mining Technicians: Operating and maintaining mining equipment.

Drilling & Blasting Engineers: Managing drilling and blasting activities for ore extraction.

Smelting & Ore Processing:

Metallurgists: Optimizing smelting and refining processes.

Process Engineers: Ensuring efficient ore processing.

Smelting Operators: Operating furnaces and smelting equipment.

Health, Safety & Environmental:

Health & Safety Managers: Ensuring safe operations and regulatory compliance.

Environmental Engineers: Managing the environmental impact of mining operations.

Safety Officers: Conducting safety audits and risk assessments.

Logistics & Supply Chain:

Supply Chain Managers: Coordinating procurement and transportation of materials.

Logistics Coordinators: Managing the movement of materials across operations.

Corporate & Support Roles:

HR Specialists: Sourcing talent to meet mining industry needs.

Finance & Accounting: Managing financial operations and reporting.

Legal & Compliance: Ensuring adherence to industry regulations.

Whether you’re in exploration, extraction, or refining, Finaxe helps you recruit the right talent to drive success in mining and metals. Let us support your team-building needs in this essential sector.
